# Strap — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Overview

Strap ("the App") is owned and operated by Jonathan Mable. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

**Information you provide:**
- Job titles, industries, and experience levels you enter during practice sessions
- Interview answers you type into the app
- Job postings you paste into the app

**Automatically collected information:**
- Anonymous usage data collected by RevenueCat for purchase processing
- No personally identifiable information is collected without your consent

## How We Use Your Information

- To generate interview questions and feedback tailored to your inputs
- To save your practice session history locally on your device
- To process in-app purchases via RevenueCat

## Data Storage

All practice session data (questions, answers, feedback) is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not transmit or store your practice data on any server.

## Third-Party Services

**Anthropic Claude API** — Your interview questions and answers are sent to Anthropic's API to generate questions and feedback. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data. See: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

**RevenueCat** — Used to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat's privacy policy applies to purchase data. See: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## Data Retention

Practice session data is stored on your device until you delete the app. You can clear your session history by deleting and reinstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

Strap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
